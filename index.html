Bio

My name is Shreyas Hampali, currently a 2nd-year PhD student in Computer Vision at the Institute of Computer Graphics and Vision (ICG), TU Graz, Austria. I am advised by Prof. Vincent Lepetit. My research interests lie in hand and object pose estimation problems from RGB/RGBD images. I am part of Computer Vision for Augmented Reality Lab at ICG.
