We introduce PALM, a large-scale hand dataset containing 13,000 3D scans and 90,000 multi-view RGB images from 263 diverse subjects. Paired with MANO registrations, PALM supports learning robust hand priors for pose estimation, hand personalization, and generative modeling. We present PALM-Net, a baseline model that learns hand geometry and material properties through physically based inverse rendering.
